Foundations Core Skills Reflections



Describe the impact the core learning has had on your Foundations learning journey.

It’s impacted my learning journey more than I thought it would. I would say it helped facilitate a better frame of mind as it was constantly reminding me of techniques to manage myself emotionally etc.



What are 1 - 3 new things you have learned about yourself as a result of the core learning?

I learnt that no matter how hard something is, that my brain gauges neuroplasticity and that with practice and perseverance I can learn to code (which I surprise myself by doing often). And that I tend to look down upon my work (in this case coding) as I completely shunned away from sharing my work via slack channel.



What has surprised you the most about the core learning?

I think what surprised me the most was the flexibility of it, that it didn’t feel like work really but more so fun and a pleasure (like sunday newspaper reading if anyone still does that).



What was the most challenging aspects of the core learning?

I guess getting over the barrier I made myself of “this is cheesy” and “I already know all of this stuff” and trying to embrace it as much as the technical part.



Any thoughts you want to share about your core learning journey in Foundations?

I would to people I get to know better in time but not right away.
