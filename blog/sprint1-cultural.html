Using the evidence gathered, describe your core personal values.

A core personal value of mine would be empathy. “Treat others the way you want to be treated” is a quote I’ve kept in mind for a very long time. I remind myself of this quote every time I am confronted with someone who is giving me a hard time (To know that they are frustrated with something, and to try empathize with them instead of fighting back with my own opinion). This keeps me from being frustrated with people even in the most tension filled times. To go along with this I am pretty optimistic with my view in life, hence I’ve been told I have a calm-demeanor a lot by friends and even my employer. This photo (will insert later) is of me and my life’s biggest role-model, my great-grandmother Tito! She is the biggest example I know of someone who works super hard for other people to benefit off of, yet she has never yearned for attention or rewards for her work. She is completely selfless and humble, and I believe that is why many people within our Niuean community love and respect her. I aspire to be as selfless and humble as her.
